Donut Shop Key Layout

  • Hover over the [C] keys to read about combos.
  • Blank keys are transparent
  • Sticky mods in pink
  • Page credits to baobaozi https://codepen.io/baobaozi/pen/mdOjpQp

Typing

Alphas follow Colemak-DH. Uses combos, thumb shift, right space.

Numbers/Symbols

Hold down the SYM key to activate this layer. Home-row numbers with shifted punctuation above and brackets and additional chars below.

Navigation

Hold down the NAV key to activate this layer. Inspired by DreymaR-style extend layer (source) with some tweaks. No mouse keys, no cut/copy/paste since it's on default layer combos. Sticky mods on left-hand bottom row.

Function

Hold down NAV and SYM keys to activate this layer. F-keys on the right hand. Sticky mods on the left hand plus RGB configuration (not shown).
